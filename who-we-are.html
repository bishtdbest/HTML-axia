Bob Ziomek

Bob provides and develops Axia’s overall approach to catastrophic cost management solutions. As co-founder of the nation’s first transplant network, Bob is the foremost expert in the design of transplant benefit management solutions on behalf of reinsurance/stop-loss carriers, health insurance carriers, health plans and large corporations.

Bob assists clients in the assessment, selection and implementation of a variety of catastrophic care solutions, including transplant network, oncology risk management, medical travel, NICU, renal dialysis, specialty contract negotiation and subrogation products. Bob has worked extensively in the health insurance industry for almost 30 years.
